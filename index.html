The Runforge Idea

Runforge is a project designed to create a roguelite in Minecraft. You will be able to join the server, get gear, and then hop into a dungeon. After completing the dungeon (or dying), you will be able to go back to spawn with new loot. At spawn, you can purchase and unlock better gear to help you beat harder dungeons. We have much planned so join the discord for future information.
